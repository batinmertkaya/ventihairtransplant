DHI Hair Transplant

DHI hair transplant is one of the most advanced and effective hair restoration techniques available today. In this procedure, individual follicular units are extracted from the donor area and directly implanted into the recipient site.

Read More

No-shave Hair Transplant

No-shave hair transplant (NSHT) treats hair loss without shaving. Follicular units are taken from the donor area and implanted into thinning or bald spots, blending naturally with existing hair. It's ideal for a natural and aesthetically pleasing result.

Learn More

Body Hair Transplant

Body hair transplant is a modern and comprehensive solution to hair loss concerns. In this procedure, individual follicular units are extracted from non-scalp areas, such as the chest, back, arms, or legs, and transplanted to bald.
